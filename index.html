Airdrop System

70,000 BLD across 3 phases. No owner, no admin, no keeper. Runs on block.timestamp alone.

01
One Wallet, One Claim
Each wallet claims exactly once for the entire contract lifetime. Permanent, no exceptions.
02
180-Day Vesting
Cycle 1 only. Vesting starts from period start. Matured portion transfers at claim, rest via withdrawVesting().
03
Rollover
Unclaimed tokens roll to the next period. Cycle 1 remainders carry into Cycle 2. Nothing is lost.
04
Dust Rule
Active on C2-P5 and Final Phase only. Last claimer receives all remaining tokens and contract closes.
05
Auto Closure
Contract closes when Dust Rule triggers. After currentPhase == 3 every call reverts.
06
Permissionless
claim(), withdrawVesting(), finalizePeriod(), startCycle2() — callable by anyone.
